About Us

Welcome to the Electrical Engineering Department. We are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service.

The Electrical Engineering department at Tolani F.G. Polytechnic, Adipur has been active in teaching since 1967. Currently, it has a fine blend of experience as well as a young and dynamic faculty, which is involved in providing quality education. Currently, almost 05 Faculties are involved in Grant in Aid Institution which has an intake of 50 students while 06 Faculties are involved in Self Finance Institution, which is started in year 2006 with an intake of 60 students. The department has different laboratories like (i) Electrical Machine Laboratory (ii) Electrical Measurement Laboratory (iii) Microprocessor Laboratory (iv) Electronics Laboratory. All Laboratories are well equipped with Electrical Machines, Practical kits, and instruments.

Faculty

Our faculty members are renowned experts in their fields, dedicated to providing quality education and conducting groundbreaking research.
