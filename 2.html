About Us

The Computer Department is dedicated to providing top-notch education and research in computer science and engineering.

  • Growth of Computer Engineering: Over the past two decades, computer engineering has transformed from a relatively nascent field into a powerful driver of progress. Its impact is felt across various domains, from everyday activities to cutting-edge economic and technological developments.

  • Ubiquity of Computation: Computers are now ubiquitous, shaping our daily lives and influencing how we interact with the world. Whether it’s through mobile devices, cloud computing, or artificial intelligence, computation is at the heart of modern society.
  • Job Market: Contrary to common misconceptions, the job market for computer technology professionals remains robust and continues to expand. As technology evolves, so do the opportunities for skilled computer engineers.
  • Innovation: Computer engineers play a pivotal role in innovating and shaping the future. Their work extends beyond theoretical knowledge to practical applications that impact society at large.
  • Mission of the Department: The Computer Engineering department at Tolani has a three-fold mission: creating knowledge, disseminating knowledge, and serving the local and broader communities. This commitment reflects the department’s dedication to education, research, and community engagement.
  • Invigorating Environment: The department is described as lively and invigorating—a place where students and faculty collaborate, learn, and contribute to the field.
  • Encouragement to Visit: As you’ve invited us to explore the department’s web presence, I encourage anyone interested in computer engineering to visit the Tolani campus and experience firsthand the dynamic environment of the Computer Engineering department.
