Welcome to the Mechanical Engineering Department

Our department is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and service. We offer a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in mechanical engineering.

About Us

The Mechanical Engineering Department at XYZ University was established in 1965 and has since been at the forefront of innovation and education. Our faculty are leaders in their fields, and our alumni are making significant contributions around the world.

Mechanical Department has established well equipped laboratories and other facilities for smooth conduction of diploma programs. Laboratory facility includes workshop, CAD/CAM Lab, Fluid Mechanics & Hydraulic Machines lab, Thermal Engg. lab, Metrology lab, Material Technology Lab. One of the feature of the laboratory is enhancement of facilities through students project. Experienced faculty looks after the conduction of academic activities. Faculty updates themselves on regular basis. Many faculties have presented papers at national level seminars. Faculty upgrades their qualification under faculty development program. Students learning is well structured & less on plan is informed to them in advance. Students are encouraged to participate in teaching as well as soft skills enhancement activities on regular basis. Many students have won prizes at various competitions. Instructional program is well supported by experienced supporting staff in workshop and laboratories. Continues efforts and hard work of faculty and students resulted in landmark achievements such 1st at GTU results.
